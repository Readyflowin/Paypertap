# PayPerTap Email Lifecycle Plan

Implemented now: event-based transactional emails for seller welcome, store created, product added, and order notifications.

Future lifecycle emails are not implemented yet:

- Store incomplete reminder after 24 hours and 72 hours.
- Product missing reminder after 24 hours and 3 days.
- Inactive seller reminders, maximum 1 per week.
- Promotional emails, maximum 1 per week.
- Stop reminders once the target action is completed.
- Respect unsubscribe and marketing preferences before promotional or reminder emails.
